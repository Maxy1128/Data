4. Learning and Sharing Session

Description
Strengthen personal knowledge related to ongoing projects and contribute to team knowledge exchange.

	1.	Complete at least 10 hours of structured learning in areas such as data analysis, modeling methods, tools, or business domain knowledge that support current projects. (By the end of December)
	2.	Apply learning outcomes in daily work, for example improving data analysis approaches, modeling support, or process understanding. (Ongoing)
	3.	Contribute to at least one team or department knowledge sharing session to boost knowledge exchange. such as preparing materials, organizing content, or delivering part of the presentation in a formal meeting. (By the end of November)
	4.	Summarize key learning points and practical takeaways from the learning process and sharing activity. (By the end of December)
